Nikkei Progressives is a grassroots, all-volunteer, multi-generational community organization. We formed in late 2016 in response to the Trump Administration’s expected attacks on the civil liberties of Muslim Americans, immigrants and other minority groups and in recognition of the need to offer support and resistance. We care deeply about Little Tokyo and issues of justice and fairness within the Japanese American community and beyond.
